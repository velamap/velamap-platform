## What is Document QA

Document question answering (Document QA) is the task of answering natural language questions using information extracted from one or more documents. The model must locate relevant passages within potentially long documents and synthesize an accurate answer grounded in the source text. It combines information retrieval with language understanding and generation.

## Core Ideas

Document QA systems typically follow a retrieve-then-read pipeline: a retriever finds relevant passages, and a reader model generates an answer conditioned on those passages. With long-context models, the entire document can sometimes be placed directly in the context window, eliminating the retrieval step. Grounding answers in source text is critical for factual accuracy and auditability.

## Why It Matters

Organizations hold vast amounts of knowledge in PDFs, reports, contracts, and wikis that are difficult to query programmatically. Document QA makes this knowledge accessible through natural language, enabling faster research, compliance checking, and customer support. It is a foundational capability for enterprise AI applications built on proprietary document collections.
