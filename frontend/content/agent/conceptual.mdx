## What It Is
An AI agent is a system where an LLM acts as a reasoning engine that can take actions, observe results, and iterate toward a goal. Unlike a single-turn chatbot, an agent operates in a loop: perceive, reason, act, observe. Agents can use tools, browse the web, write and execute code, and interact with external systems.

## Core Idea
The LLM serves as the "brain" of the agent, deciding what action to take next based on the current state and goal. Actions are executed by tools or APIs, and their results are fed back into the LLM's context. This loop continues until the goal is achieved or a stopping condition is met.

## Why It Matters
Agents extend LLMs from passive text generators to active problem solvers that can interact with the world. They can complete tasks that require multiple steps, external information, or real-world actions. Agents are the foundation of autonomous AI systems for software engineering, research, and business process automation.
